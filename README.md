Project Overview
This project provides a comprehensive look at organizational health by analyzing employee turnover and satisfaction levels. By synthesizing complex HR data, the dashboard allows stakeholders to pinpoint exactly where and why attrition is occurring.

Executive Summary
This project features a comprehensive HR Analytics Dashboard designed to monitor workforce stability and identify financial risks associated with employee turnover. By analyzing a dataset of 1,470 employees, the dashboard provides a high-level view of key performance indicators (KPIs) such as attrition rates, job satisfaction, and demographic trends to support data-driven decision-making for HR and Finance departments.

Financial & Operational KPIs
Attrition Rate (16.12%): Monitored overall turnover to assess the impact on recruitment costs and operational stability.
Workforce Demographics: Analyzed an average employee age of 37 and segmented 1,233 active employees to understand long-term organizational health.
Departmental Risk Assessment: Identified that the R&D department faces the highest attrition risk at 56.12%, signaling a potential area for targeted retention strategies.
Job Satisfaction Correlation: Developed a heat map correlating roles with satisfaction ratings (1–4) to pinpoint departments with low engagement that could lead to future financial losses.
